# IM Troubleshooting

## Telegram bot does not reply

Check service health, env file location, webhook secret consistency, and IM logs.

## DingTalk messages are not received

Check whether DingTalk is enabled, whether Stream credentials are correct, and whether the bot is mentioned in group chats when required.

## `/help` works but normal text does not

Verify provider settings and inspect logs for provider-disabled or routing-related errors.
