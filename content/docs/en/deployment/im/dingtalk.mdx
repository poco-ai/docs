# DingTalk Integration

DingTalk supports two inbound modes in Poco.

## Stream mode

Recommended when you do not want to depend on a public webhook.

## Webhook mode

Use when you prefer callback-based delivery to `/api/v1/webhooks/dingtalk`.

## Extra outbound fallback

You can also configure `DINGTALK_WEBHOOK_URL` as a fallback outbound notification channel.
